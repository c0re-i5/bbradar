## Ethical Guidelines

If you are performing security research, we strongly encourage you to:

- **Follow responsible disclosure practices.** Report vulnerabilities to the
  affected organization before publishing details.
- **Minimize impact.** Avoid actions that could degrade service availability,
  expose user data, or cause harm beyond what is necessary to demonstrate a
  vulnerability.
- **Respect privacy.** Do not access, store, or share personal data obtained
  during testing beyond what is strictly necessary to document a finding.
- **Document everything.** Maintain clear records of your authorization, scope,
  methodology, and findings.
- **Stay within scope.** If a bug bounty program defines specific targets and
  exclusions, adhere to them strictly.
